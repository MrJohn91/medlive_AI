# MedLive AI

**Reducing healthcare wait times with an AI triage assistant that sees, listens, and guides patients to the right care.**

## The Problem

Millions of patients face long wait times and uncertainty when seeking medical care. They don't know if their symptoms need emergency attention, a doctor's visit, or simple home care. Clinics are overwhelmed with calls, and patients often end up in the wrong care setting.

## The Solution

MedLive AI is a voice-powered medical triage assistant with a friendly avatar that:

- **Listens** to patients describe their symptoms naturally
- **Sees** visible symptoms through the camera (rashes, swelling, wounds)
- **Guides** patients to the right level of care (ER, urgent care, or home treatment)

## Built With

- Google Gemini 2.0 (Live API + Vision)
- LiveKit (real-time communication)
- Anam (lifelike avatar)
- Google Cloud Platform

## Submission

This project is a submission for the [Gemini Live Agent Challenge](https://geminiliveagentchallenge.devpost.com/).
