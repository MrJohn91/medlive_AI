### SHOWING YOUR SYMPTOM (Vision Demo)

*If she asks to see it, hold up your arm to the camera (show the rash mark).*

*Let her describe what she sees; don't interrupt.*

### SYMPTOM QUESTIONS

*When she asks when it started:*
